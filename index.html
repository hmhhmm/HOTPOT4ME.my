Why HOTPOT4ME

Everything wrong with group hotpot, fixed.

Traditional steamboat means shared broth, mismatched cravings and waiting on the table. We rebuilt it around one person — you.

🍲

Your own pot

A private mini-hotpot per diner. No shared utensils, no cross-contamination — the post-COVID hygiene worry, gone.

🎛️

Full customization

Choose up to two broths, then mix proteins, veg, carbs and sauces. Spicy, vegetarian, low-sodium — entirely your call.

☪️

Halal & inclusive

Fully JAKIM-halal so Muslim and non-Muslim friends finally share the same table without compromise.

⚡

Quick & affordable

The boundary-crossing layer

A hotpot that thinks with you.

We didn't just digitise the menu. Three connected technologies turn a meal into a guided, data-aware experience.

AI
🤖

AI Pot Genie

Tell it how you feel — “spicy & high-protein” or “light, low-sodium, halal” — and it designs a complete pot from our live menu in seconds.

Removes: decision fatigue & long menu scrolling at the table.
IoT
🌡️

Smart temperature pots

Each induction pot self-regulates — gentle simmer, rolling boil or keep-warm — so beef never overcooks and broth never boils dry.

Removes: guesswork, scorched food & safety risk.
DATA
📊

Live nutrition tracking

As you build, the app totals calories, protein, carbs, fat and sodium per pot — so health-conscious diners eat with full transparency.

Removes: the blind-eating problem for fitness & diet goals.
